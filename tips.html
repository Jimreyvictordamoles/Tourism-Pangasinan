Travel Tips

Helpful guides for visiting Pangasinan safely and smoothly

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Pangasinan is during the dry season from November to May. This is ideal for beach trips, island hopping, and outdoor activities since the weather is sunny and the seas are calm. Summer months (March to May) are the most popular for tourists, especially in Hundred Islands and Bolinao.

Transportation Options

You can reach Pangasinan by bus from Manila going to Dagupan, Alaminos, or Bolinao. Within the province, tricycles, jeepneys, and vans are the main transportation. For island destinations, local boats are available for tours and transfers.

Safety Reminders

Always follow safety rules when swimming or island hopping. Wear life vests during boat rides, avoid deep waters if you are not a strong swimmer, and listen to local guides. Keep your belongings secure, especially in crowded tourist areas.

Budget Planning

Pangasinan is a budget-friendly destination. Most beaches have low entrance fees, and food is affordable in local markets. However, island hopping tours and private resorts may cost more, so plan your expenses ahead of time.

Local Etiquette & Respect

Respect local culture and traditions, especially when visiting churches and rural areas. Dress modestly in religious sites like Manaoag Church, and always ask permission before taking photos of locals. Being polite goes a long way in Pangasinan communities.
